Eduation History

Education

I received my Ph.D. on May 12, 2018 from the Department of Mathematics at the University of South Carolina under the direction of Matthew Ballard. My dissertation is titled Geometry of Derived Categories on Noncommutative Projective Schemes.

Learn More

About my work

Research

I work in noncommutative projective algebraic geometry at the intersection of the styles of Kontsevich and Artin-Zhang. In particular, I study derived categories associated to noncommutative projective schemes with an emphasis on importing methods of projective algebraic geometry into the study of noncommutative graded algebras.
