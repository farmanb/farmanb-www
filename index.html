About

I am a sixth year Ph.D. candidate in the Deparment of Mathematics at the University of South Carolina. I am expected to graduate in May 2018. My advisor is Matt Ballard.

My interests include (noncommutative) Algebraic Geometry, Category Theory, Derived Categories, and Number Theory. Currently, the focus of my research is the geometry of derived categories of noncommutative projective schemes.
